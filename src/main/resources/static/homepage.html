About MeetUp Planner

MeetUp Planner is the ultimate scheduling and location mapping platform to bring people together. We integrate the power of Google Maps API with a robust calendar and scheduling feature, making it simple and effortless to plan meetups. From coffee dates to business meetings or grand reunions, we provide a seamless and stress-free process.

Features

  • Seamless Integration with Google Maps: Easily pick your meetup location.
  • Scheduling: Schedule your meetups and invite attendees in a few clicks.
  • The More, The Merrier!: Add friends and create amazing meetups together!.
  • Reminder Notifications: Receive notifications and never miss a meetup.
  • User-friendly Interface: Enjoy a clean, intuitive, and easy-to-use interface.
  • Outstanding Customer Service: Benefit from responsive support, with assistance for password resets and more, ensuring you have the best user experience possible.
  • State-of-the-art Email Verification: Enhance your account security with our state-of-the-art email verification process. With us, it's not just about meeting up, it's about peace of mind.
